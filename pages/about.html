About

My Journey

I'm a passionate web developer with a background in computer science and over 3 years of experience building user-friendly, accessible web applications. My journey started in the university where I fell in love with the creative problem-solving aspect of programming.

Over the years, I've worked with various technologies including React, Vue.js, and modern CSS frameworks. I believe in creating web experiences that are not only visually appealing but also inclusive and accessible to everyone.

Goals in This Program

My primary goals for this program include:

  • Mastering advanced frontend development patterns and best practices
  • Improving my understanding of web accessibility standards and implementation
  • Enhancing my skills in responsive design and cross browser compatibility
  • Learning to build more efficient and maintainable codebases
  • Developing expertise in performance optimization techniques

I'm particularly excited about the opportunity to work on real-world projects and receive feedback from experienced developers in the field.

Areas of Low Confidence

While I'm comfortable with many aspects of frontend development, there are areas where I'd like to build more confidence:

  • Advanced javascript logic: I understand the basics but want to master how to handle and debug it
  • Web accessibility implementation: I know the principles but want more practice with complex ARIA patterns
  • Performance optimization: I can identify performance issues but want to get better at preventing them
  • Testing strategies: I want to improve my skills in writing comprehensive unit and integration tests

I'm looking forward to addressing these areas through hands-on practice from this program.

Note to Future Self

Remember why you started this journey. The late nights debugging, the moments of frustration when something wouldn't work, and the exhilaration when it finally did all of these experiences are shaping you into a better developer.

Don't lose sight of the joy in creating things that solve real problems. Keep learning, stay curious, and don't be afraid to ask for help when you need it. The tech landscape will continue to evolve, and your ability to adapt will be your greatest strength.

Additional Thoughts

I'm particularly passionate about the intersection of technology and social impact. I believe that as developers, we have a responsibility to create digital experiences that are inclusive, ethical, and beneficial to society.

In the future, I hope to contribute to projects that address real world challenges like education accessibility, and healthcare innovation. I'm excited about the potential of technology to make positive changes in people's lives.
